Clutch Connections

Build a 5-man starting lineup of NBA legends — chained by their initials.

  1. You start with two letters. Your first pick's first name and last name must match them.
  2. The last name initial of each player becomes the first name initial of your next pick.
  3. Fill all five positions — PG, SG, SF, PF, C. Each player can only take a spot they actually played.
  4. Once you pick a player you're committed — you can change their position, but can't swap them out. Choose carefully.
  5. Your score is the sum of each player's best single-season PRA (points + rebounds + assists).
  6. One lineup a day. Same letters for everyone, everywhere.
Example — letters L J:
LeBron James → James Harden → Hakeem Olajuwon → Oscar Robertson → Russell Westbrook
